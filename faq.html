Frequently Asked Questions

What is Leela?

Leela is an ancient Indian game of self-knowledge, also known as the "Game of Life". It has been used for centuries as a toolkit for self-reflection, helping players understand their inner states and life paths.

How does the LeelaClue app work?

The app simplifies the complex Leela map into intuitive reflection rituals. You can draw cards that represent different states of consciousness, helping you find clarity and focus in your daily life.

Is the app free?

Yes, LeelaClue is free to download and use for your daily reflections. We believe in making this ancient wisdom accessible to everyone.

Who is behind LeelaClue?

LeelaClue is developed with passion and respect for the Vedic tradition, aiming to bridge ancient wisdom with modern digital mindfulness needs.
