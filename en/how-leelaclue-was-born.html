From Intuition to Algorithm: How LeelaClue Was Born

2026-03-25

As a facilitator of the game of Leela, I have witnessed countless times how people sit before the board, deeply moved. But I also saw a challenge: How can we keep the profound symbolism of the 72 fields alive in our everyday lives? How can we have the "clues" that the game gives us ready at any time?

From this question, LeelaClue was born.

A Digital Compass for an Ancient Journey

The idea for LeelaClue was not born at a desk, but directly on the playing field.

I observed how players, after a session, often felt the need to look up the meaning of a specific field – be it Krodha (Anger) or Dharma (Destiny) – once again or to take it with them as a daily impulse.

LeelaClue is more than just an app – it is a digital companion for your transformation.

What LeelaClue Offers: The Clue from the Field

The heart of the app is the connection between the game board and your current life situation.

  • Understanding the Fields: Each of the 72 fields is complex. LeelaClue breaks down Vedic wisdom into a language we understand today.
  • Synchronicity in Everyday Life: Even if you are not currently at a physical board, the app allows you to receive a "Clue from the Field." It's like rolling the dice for a moment to get an intuitive answer to a pressing question.
  • Personal Guidance: I am personally by your side if you cannot understand the reading after all.
  • Journaling / Diary: Record your insights to better understand your development. And much more...

Why Technology and Spirituality are not a Contradiction

Some ask me: "Does an app fit such an old, sacred game?"

My answer is a clear yes. In 1977, the year I was born, computers were only just starting to conquer our daily lives. Today we use them for everything – so why not for our personal development as well?

LeelaClue uses technology not for distraction, but for focus. It serves as a bridge. When you look at your phone in the stress of everyday life and read a clue from a field like "Peace," it immediately brings you back to your center.

My Conclusion as Founder and Facilitator:

LeelaClue is my gift to everyone who wants to walk the path of self-knowledge. It does not replace the deep experience of a guided session on the field, but it supports you in integrating the magic of Leela into every minute of your life.

The game does not stop when you leave the board. With LeelaClue, you always have your guide in your pocket.
