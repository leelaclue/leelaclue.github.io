3. Clues — Virtual Currency Model

LeelaClue operates on a virtual-currency model. Most features are free; AI-assisted features ("Leela Guru") are unlocked using Clues, an in-app virtual currency.

  • Free Access: Drawing cards, recording reflections, daily wisdom rituals, the Leela Field view, and reading past spreads are always free.
  • Welcome Gift: New users receive a one-time welcome gift of Clues on first launch.
  • Paid Features: Asking the Leela Guru to refine your intent, personalize your guidance questions, generate practice suggestions, or summarize a spread requires Clues.

Acquiring Clues

Clues are sold as one-time consumable in-app purchases on the Dakshina screen. The pricing tiers shown in the app are the authoritative source.

Note on pricing: Prices are displayed in the local currency of your App Store / Google Play account. The exact amount charged may vary slightly depending on regional tax rules and platform-specific exchange rates set by Apple and Google.

Refunds

Clues are a non-transferable, non-refundable virtual licence with no cash value. Once acquired, Clues are not refundable by LeelaClue. Refunds, where applicable, are governed exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play policies and must be requested directly from Apple or Google.

Protecting Clues with Your Apple or Google Account

You can optionally sign in with your Apple or Google account on the Dakshina screen to protect purchased Clues. Sign-in is never required to use the application.

  • One balance per account: Purchased Clues are linked to the signed-in Apple or Google account. Each account holds exactly one Clues balance, shared by all devices signed in with that account.
  • Restore on a new device: Signing in with the same account on a new device or after a reinstall restores the purchased balance to that device.
  • Welcome-gift Clues do not transfer: The one-time welcome gift is tied to the installation on which it was granted. Gift Clues are not restored on other devices or merged across accounts; this is an abuse-prevention measure.
  • Switching accounts: Signing in with a different account on a device switches the device to that account's balance. The previous balance is not lost — it remains with the previous account and becomes available again when you sign in with it.

Loss of Clues

Without sign-in, the Clues balance is tied to an anonymous UUID stored on your device. If you uninstall the application, reset your device, clear app data, or change devices without restoring your data, the Clues balance associated with that UUID may become permanently inaccessible. Purchased Clues can only be recovered if you signed in with your Apple or Google account before losing access; welcome-gift Clues cannot be recovered in any case.

No Guarantee on Pricing or Availability

We may change Clue prices, package contents, or the AI features Clues unlock, without prior notice. Existing balances remain spendable on whichever features are then available.
