Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms is LeelaClue available on?

LeelaClue is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). The app works on smartphones and tablets. You can also explore the Leela board game using our Offline Game guide on this website.

Is LeelaClue free to use?

Yes, the app is completely free with no hidden subscriptions or in-app purchases. All features — including Daily Wisdom, Daily Guidance, the Spiritual Diary, and the full 72-card deck — are available at no cost.

Does LeelaClue collect my personal data?

No. LeelaClue does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your reflections and journal entries remain 100% local on your device. There are no analytics trackers, no accounts, and no cloud storage. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What languages does LeelaClue support?

LeelaClue supports English, German, and Russian. The app automatically detects your device language and switches accordingly. All 72 card descriptions, daily practices, and interface elements are fully translated in each language.

How do the daily rituals work?

LeelaClue offers two daily rituals:

  • Daily Wisdom: Presents a single card with a practice for the day, designed for morning intention-setting.
  • Daily Guidance (3-Card Spread): A meditative spread for exploring your most important life questions. It reveals your current state, hidden influences, and guidance.

Each ritual can be used once per day to encourage deep reflection rather than habitual checking. Learn more in the User Guide.

What is Leela?

Leela is an ancient Indian game of self-knowledge, also known as the "Game of Life" or "Snakes and Arrows". It has been used for centuries as a tool for spiritual self-reflection. The 72 spaces on the game board represent different states of consciousness, from Anger and Jealousy to Bliss and Cosmic Consciousness.

What is the Offline Leela Game?

The Offline Leela Game is a physical version of Leela that can be played with real cards and a game board, often guided by an experienced Leela coach. We provide information about sessions and coaches on our Offline Game page.
