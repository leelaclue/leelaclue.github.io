Frequently Asked Questions

What is Leela?

Leela is an ancient Indian game of self-knowledge, also known as the "Game of Life". It has been used for centuries as a toolkit for self-reflection.

How does LeelaClue work?

The app simplifies the complex Leela map into intuitive reflection rituals using symbolism of snakes and arrows.
