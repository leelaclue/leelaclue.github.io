Brief Description of the App

LeelaClue is an entertainment app that makes the knowledge of the Leela map accessible. "The 72 fields of the Leela map are the keys to the states of your consciousness. But the map itself is often too complex. LeelaClue distills this ancient knowledge and transforms each state into a clear, intuitive message for your here and now."

What Data is Collected?

None. The app does not collect, gather, store, or transmit any personal data from users. In particular, no names, email addresses, usage data, location data, contacts, photos, audio recordings, or other personal information is transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Permissions and Access

The app does not require any special system permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, location, or contacts).

Third Parties and Third-Party Services

Currently, LeelaClue does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or cloud services that collect or transmit user data.

Storage and Transmission

Since no data is collected, there is also no storage on remote servers and no transmission to third parties.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. No personal data from children is collected.
