Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 11, 2025 This Privacy Policy informs you about the processing of personal data in connection with the LeelaClue mobile application. The protection of your personal data is important to the developer. Below you will find all relevant information about data collection, processing, and your rights. ## Responsible Party **Responsible:** Irene Engelko **Address:** Bad Schönborn, Germany **Email:** [leelaclue@gmail.com](mailto:leelaclue@gmail.com) ## Brief Description of the App LeelaClue is an entertainment app that makes the knowledge of the Leela map accessible. "The 72 fields of the Leela map are the keys to the states of your consciousness. But the map itself is often too complex. LeelaClue distills this ancient knowledge and transforms each state into a clear, intuitive message for your here and now." ## What Data is Collected? **None.** The app does not collect, gather, store, or transmit any personal data from users. In particular, no names, email addresses, usage data, location data, contacts, photos, audio recordings, or other personal information is transmitted to the developer or third parties. ## Permissions and Access The app does not require any special system permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, location, or contacts). Should a permission be required in a future version, this will be clearly communicated in the app and in this Privacy Policy. ## Third Parties and Third-Party Services Currently, LeelaClue does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or cloud services that collect or transmit user data. Should this change in the future, the Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and users will be informed. ## Storage and Transmission Since no data is collected, there is also no storage on remote servers and no transmission to third parties. ## Children The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. No personal data from children is collected. Parents and guardians who have concerns may contact the developer at the email address above. ## Rights of Data Subjects Because LeelaClue does not process personal data, there are currently no circumstances that would require a request for information or deletion under Articles 15-17 GDPR. Should data processing change in the future, the statutory rights of data subjects apply (information, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection). Please direct inquiries by email to [leelaclue@gmail.com](mailto:leelaclue@gmail.com). ## Changes to this Privacy Policy Changes or additions to this Privacy Policy will be published here. It is recommended to review the policy regularly. In case of significant changes, users will be informed in the app or via update notice. ## Note for Google Play Store – Data Safety The following information is relevant for the Google Play "Data Safety" form. Please use this summary when filling out the Play Console form: - Data collection: No — the app does not collect personal data. - Data sharing with third parties: No. - Data storage on servers: No. - Third-party SDKs (Analytics, Advertising, Crash Reporting): None currently. - Persons under 13 years: App not for children under 13. ## Contact For questions about the Privacy Policy or data handling, you can reach the developer at: **Irene Engelko** Bad Schönborn, Germany Email: [leelaclue@gmail.com](mailto:leelaclue@gmail.com) **Date:** December 11, 2025 This Privacy Policy was created specifically for the LeelaClue app. It serves as guidance for users and for submission to app stores (e.g., Google Play).
