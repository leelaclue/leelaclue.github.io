Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy informs you about the processing of personal data in connection with the LeelaClue mobile application and website. The protection of your personal data is important to the developer. Below you will find all relevant information about data collection, processing, and your rights.

Responsible Party

Responsible: Irene Engelko
Address: Bad Schönborn, Germany
Email: leelaclue@gmail.com

Brief Description of the App

LeelaClue is an entertainment app that makes the knowledge of the Leela map accessible. "The 72 fields of the Leela map are the keys to the states of your consciousness. But the map itself is often too complex. LeelaClue distills this ancient knowledge and transforms each state into a clear, intuitive message for your here and now."

What Data is Collected?

None. The app does not collect, gather, store, or transmit any personal data from users. In particular, no names, email addresses, usage data, location data, contacts, photos, audio recordings, or other personal information is transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Permissions and Access

The app does not require any special system permissions (e.g., camera, microphone, location, or contacts). Should a permission be required in a future version, this will be clearly communicated in the app and in this Privacy Policy.

Third Parties and Third-Party Services

Currently, LeelaClue does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or cloud services that collect or transmit user data. Should this change in the future, the Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and users will be informed.

Storage and Local Technologies

LeelaClue does not use traditional tracking or marketing cookies. We use Local Storage (a technology similar to cookies) solely for functional purposes:

  • Language Preference: To remember whether you prefer to view the site in English, German, or Russian.
  • Voting: To ensure that users can only vote once per feature in the Feature Voting section. Vote tokens are stored locally on your device.

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers (except for the vote choice itself which is sent to Google Forms if you choose to vote).

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. No personal data from children is collected. Parents and guardians who have concerns may contact the developer at the email address above.

Rights of Data Subjects

Because LeelaClue does not process personal data, there are currently no circumstances that would require a request for information or deletion under Articles 15-17 GDPR. Should data processing change in the future, the statutory rights of data subjects apply (information, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection). Please direct inquiries by email to leelaclue@gmail.com.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Changes or additions to this Privacy Policy will be published here. It is recommended to review the policy regularly. In case of significant changes, users will be informed in the app or via update notice.

Note for Google Play Store – Data Safety

The following information is relevant for the Google Play "Data Safety" form. Please use this summary when filling out the Play Console form:

  • Data collection: No — the app does not collect personal data.
  • Data sharing with third parties: No.

  • Third-party SDKs (Analytics, Advertising, Crash Reporting): None currently.
  • Persons under 13 years: App not for children under 13.
