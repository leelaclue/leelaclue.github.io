Leela: Why I Facilitate This Game – And Why It Can Change Your Life

2026-03-09

Many ask me: "Isn't Leela just a board game?" My answer to that is always a smile, because I know what really happens on this board.

For me, Leela is not a pastime – it is a sacred mirror that I hold up to my clients' souls.

My Own Journey: The Astonishment of the First Hour

Before I became a facilitator myself, I stood on the other side of the board. I still remember my first game exactly. I thought I would roll the dice a little. Instead, the emotional impact hit me completely unprepared.

I was stunned by the deep feelings Leela brought up in me. It was a mixture of shock over old, repressed themes and a huge relief as blockages finally dissolved. Tears flowed, there were moments of silence. In the end, there was a deep peace. This experience was the reason why I decided to accompany others on this journey.

Why Facilitation Makes the Difference

You can play Leela alone, but from my experience as a facilitator I know: Playing with a guide is many times more effective.

Why Do I Guide You?

  • The Safe Anchor: The feelings Leela triggers can be overwhelming. It is easy to lose yourself in them or flee from the truth. I am your anchor who holds you when the waves are high.
  • Illuminating the Blind Spot: We all have topics we like to overlook. As a facilitator, I help you decode the messages behind the "snakes" and "arrows" that you might try to rationalize away on your own.
  • Integration: An insight on the board is valuable, but only its implementation in everyday life brings true transformation. I help you build the bridge from the game into your life.

The Goal: A Liberated Self

When my clients stand up from the board after a session, I often see the same expression in their eyes that I had back then: A deep sigh of relief. The pressure in the chest is gone, the view of one's own life is clear and vast.

Leela shows us that we are not our problems. We are the players learning to dance with the ups and downs of life.

Are You Ready for Your Journey?

If you feel it is time not only to see the patterns of your life, but to transform them, I will gladly accompany you. In a protected space, we will walk the path across the board together – layer by layer, until you arrive back at your true core.

Send me a message if you have questions or want to start your first journey with me.
