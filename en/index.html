LeelaClue

LeelaClue – 72 gates to self-reflection. Symbolic cards for mindfulness, inner clarity, and conscious perspectives in everyday life.

Clarity begins in stillness — LeelaClue supports mindful reflection.

In everyday life, it's easy to lose perspective when thoughts circle or emotions feel blocked. LeelaClue is a calm digital space for self-reflection and mindfulness. The app uses the symbolic imagery of the ancient game of Leela to invite new perspectives and deepen personal awareness.

Your personal reflection ritual:

  • Find your focus – Formulate a question or intention that currently occupies your thoughts.
  • The 3-card reflection – Draw three cards intuitively, each offering a different perspective.

LeelaClue helps you organize thoughts and experience your own inner perception more consciously.
