LeelaClue v1.6.8

Stop Wasting Your Mental Energy on Unclear Decisions — Train Your Mind for Clarity.

Do you ever feel like you are moving through life without a map? LeelaClue is your guide to turning mental noise into signal. Rooted in the ancient wisdom of Leela, this is not just an app; it is a tool for the person who wants to stay grounded and act with intention.

Master Your Day with Two Rituals

Daily Wisdom: Stop reacting to the world and start leading your day. Set your morning intention and receive a Daily Practice designed to transform your mindset. Track your progress and watch your journey statistics grow.

Daily Guidance (The 3-Card Spread): For your most important life questions, our signature meditative spread reveals your current inner state, identifies hidden influences, and provides guidance toward your own resources.

A High-Performance Diary for Your Soul

  • Personal Evolution Map: Capture your reflections directly on each card to see exactly how your perspective evolves over time.
  • Slow Tech Philosophy: We limit rituals to once per day to help you avoid distraction and encourage deep integration of every insight.
  • Effortless Experience: Immersive design and sensory cues (like gongs and bells) keep your brain calm and focused.

For more details on how to use these features, check out our comprehensive User Guide or read about the philosophy behind the app on our Community Blog.

Support for Your Interpretation

Sometimes a reading can be deep or complex, especially when navigating the intricate symbolism of the 72 gates. For those seeking a deeper dive, experienced Leela coaches offer voluntary interpretation support. Learn more about professional guidance and our physical game sessions on the Offline Leela Game page.

Please note that this support is voluntary and depends entirely on coach availability.

Is LeelaClue for You?

LeelaClue is designed for those who refuse to let life happen to them by accident. It is your companion if you are:

  • A Seeker of Mindfulness: Looking for a structured way to anchor your day in presence.
  • A Dedicated Journaler: Wanting to track your inner growth and spiritual evolution.
  • A Pro-active Individual: Aiming to train your mind for clarity and intentional action.

Note: LeelaClue is a tool for self-discovery and introspection. It does not predict the future or provide professional medical advice. The application provides the mirror; you provide the courage to look.
