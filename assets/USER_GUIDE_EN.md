## How to Use LeelaClue

### The Main Screen
The main screen consists of:
-   **Question Input**: A text field where you type your intention or question.
-   **Hold Button**: The central button used to focus and start the spread.
-   **Help Icon (Left)**: Access instructions and app info.
-   **Diary Button (Right)**: A button with a book icon to view your past spreads.

### Performing a Spread
1.  **Formulate a Question**: Type a question or intention into the text box. It must be at least **5 words** long to encourage specific and thoughtful inquiry.
    *   *Tip: Avoid simple Yes/No questions. Ask "What..." or "How..."*
2.  **Focus & Hold**: Press and **hold** the central button.
    *   A breathing circle will appear.
    *   Inhale and exhale in sync with the circle to calm your mind.
    *   Hold the button until the circle completes its cycle. This ensures you are mentally present.
3.  **Reveal Your Cards**: Once the focus is complete, 3 cards will appear face down.
    *   Tap each card to reveal it.
    *   The app will guide you through the reveal animation.

### Interpreting Results
After revealing all 3 cards, you will see the **Result Screen**. The cards correspond to three perspectives:
1.  **Where am I?**: Your current state or the core of the issue.
2.  **What I don't notice**: Hidden influences or subconscious factors.
3.  **What will help**: Guidance, resources, or actions to take.

Tap on any card to view it in full screen and read its detailed description and theme questions.

## Features

### Top Menu
The top bar provides quick access to essential information:
-   **Question Mark (?)**: Opens the full Instructions page.
-   **Info Icon (i)**: Access legal information like Impressum and Disclaimer.
-   **Mail Icon**: Opens the Contact page with social media links.
-   **Flag Icon**: Changes the app language.

### My Diary
All your completed spreads are automatically saved to your **Diary**.
-   Tap the **Diary Button** (book icon) on the main screen to open the Diary.
-   Review past questions and the cards you drew.
-   Reflect on how your situation has evolved.

### Sharing Your Insights
You can share your spread with friends or save it for later.
-   On the Result screen or in the Diary, tap the **Share** button.
-   The app generates a beautiful image of your spread including your question and the cards.
-   Share it via WhatsApp, Telegram, Save to Files, or other apps.


### Daily Limit
LeelaClue is designed for deep reflection, not casual gaming.
-   **One Spread Per Day**: You are limited to one new spread per day.
-   This limit encourages you to sit with the answer and truly integrate the insight before asking again.
-   You can review your *History* (Diary) as many times as you like.

## Example Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Career Decisions
**Situation**: You have a new job offer but are unsure about leaving your current stable job.
**Question**: *"What should I consider regarding the new job offer at TechCorp?"*
**The Spread**:
1.  *Card 1 (Where am I?)*: **Insecurity**. You might be feeling anxious about your skills.
2.  *Card 2 (What I don't notice)*: **Sudharma (Right Action)**. You are actually aligned with your purpose, and this move is part of it.
3.  *Card 3 (What will help)*: **Jnana (Knowledge)**. Gather more facts about the new role to calm your fears.

### Scenario 2: Relationship Conflict
**Situation**: You had a fight with a close friend and don't know how to fix it.
**Question**: *"How can I resolve the misunderstanding with my friend Sarah?"*
**The Spread**:
1.  *Card 1 (Where am I?)*: **Ego**. You might be holding onto pride.
2.  *Card 2 (What I don't notice)*: **Attachment**. You are afraid of losing the friendship, which is making you defensive.
3.  *Card 3 (What will help)*: **Compassion**. Approach Sarah with empathy for her feelings, dropping the need to be "right".

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I ask another question today?**
A: To preserve the quality of your reflection, the app limits spreads to one per day. Take time to meditate on today's answer.


**Q: Can I delete a diary entry?**
A: Yes, you can delete an entry by swiping it to the left in the Diary screen. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
