## Meet Anna

Anna is anxious about leaving her corporate job to go freelance. She frames it as confusion — "I just don't know what to do." She draws three cards.

> "She wasn't confused. She was protecting a familiar identity — the responsible daughter who doesn't take stupid risks."

**Her daily discipline:** 15 minutes outlining her business plan *before* opening corporate email. A small boundary drawn between the person she was and the person she was becoming.
