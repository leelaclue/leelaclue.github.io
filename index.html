LeelaClue

The Ancient Game of Self-Knowledge

LeelaClue is a tool for self-discovery and mindfulness, based on the ancient Indian game of Leela. It helps you navigate life's challenges by offering clarity through the symbolism of snakes and arrows.

Explore your inner world, understand your patterns, and find guidance in this digital adaptation of the Vedic tradition.
